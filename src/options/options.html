Menu & layout

Moves the workspace tabs (Chat, Agent…) to the left or right inside the sidebar. The sidebar's own left/right position is set by the browser, not the extension (Firefox: right-click the sidebar header → « Move sidebar to the right »; Chrome's side panel is right-only). Tip: the ⤢ « open in a tab » button gives a full-screen view you can place anywhere.

Workspace icons to show

Untick a workspace to hide its icon from the tabs menu. You can also drag the icons in the sidebar to reorder them. Tip: right-click a tab in the sidebar to hide it.
